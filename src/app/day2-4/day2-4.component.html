SPIRITUAL IMPARTATION

A Moment of Prayer

Prayer has a way of allowing the Holy Spirit to form in our hearts Christian values, virtues, and principles, for them to be noticeable in our living lifestyle.

You are now going to talk to God concerning growth in your personal relationship with Him.

 Prayer Points

  • Thank God for the gift of His personal influence in your life
  • Pray, requesting that He will continue to open your eyes to see His personal influence in your life
  • Pray, requesting that He will open your eyes to continue to see Him as your source of life
  • Pray, requesting that the Holy Spirit will build in you a condition of dependability on God
  • Worship the Lord for granting your requests

Listening to Hear from God

Please remain quiet and listen to hear God speak to you in your heart concerning what you have read about Him, and what you have talked to Him about, and/or what He would like you to know and or to do.

Notes

  • In your journal, please write down what in your heart you heard God spoke to you about.
  •   Using Google Search, or any other efficient Search tool, please find and write down a corresponding scripture or bible verse to what you heard God spoke to you about in your heart
