  • False

  • A. Going to the battle with his 318 trained men, whom he armed
  • B. Melchizedek helping him to know that the winning of his battle against the kings was by the personal help of God
  • C. Following his tithing, he became wealthy in gold and cattle

3). As the father of the Church, it is Abraham’s tithing that gives us the foundation and the legitimacy for Church tithing.

  • True
  • False
