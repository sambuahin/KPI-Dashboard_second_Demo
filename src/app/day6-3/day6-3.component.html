DAY 6: THE CHURCH TITHING CREED

My Father, the Lord, the Most-High God The possessor of heaven and earth Your most holy name be praised forevermore I bring you my tithes in honor of your holy name And by my tithes, I acknowledge you as my personal source

Through my work to put bread on the table And to meet other needs in my life In tithing, I dedicate to you my spirit, soul, and body In tithing, I consecrate myself to you, most Holy Father While I renounce my friendship with the world and its lust

I petition that you will daily fill my life with the Holy Spirit That I may grow in knowing you and your Holy Son, Jesus Christ

To whom I tithe, as the High Priest of the Most-High God To His Church, and to me, a member of His Holy Church

And for the furtherance of your kingdom, my Father Grant me an abiding personal relationship with you To help my trust, my love, and my holiness to you For yours is the kingdom, the power, and the glory Forever and ever.
